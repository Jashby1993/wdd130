My name is Justin Ashby, and I am just now getting back to college after a seven year break. I decided to go back to college because I wanted to make a career change, and I took out a few library books to see what I thought, and felt prompted that this is the right thing to do now!

But with three kids under three, I don't have time for much of that, except the last one. I also played double bass from middle school to college, and the dream is to get back into that. I also enjoy playing backyard sports: ultimate frisbee, three on three football, and driveway basketball. My favorite place to hike is American Fork Canyon, or by Bridal Veil Falls in Provo Canyon.
